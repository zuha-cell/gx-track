🛡️ Guardian X

Personal safety app for Android. One tap sends GPS + emergency call to your contacts. No account. No server. Works offline. 168+ countries.

v2.0 Android Kotlin + Jetpack Compose No server No login

Features

🆘 SOS Button Free

Press the red button — calls your chosen contact, sends GPS location by SMS to all contacts, activates siren and flash strobe. One STOP button cancels everything.

📳 Shake to SOS Free

Shake your phone 3 times — triggers full SOS even when the screen is locked, face-down, or in your pocket. Configurable sensitivity in Settings.

⏰ Check-In Alert Free

Set a repeating timer (1 minute to 12 hours). At each interval, a notification asks "Are you OK?" — tap I'm OK to reset, or let it fire SOS automatically after 5 minutes of no response. Home screen widget included for quick check-ins.

🚗 Journey Mode Free

Enter your destination and expected travel time. At arrival, confirm safe arrival or extend +15/+30 minutes. No confirmation in 5 minutes → SOS fires automatically.

🤫 Panic Word Free

Set a secret word in Settings → Panic Word. While in Ghost Mode (Notepad), type that exact word anywhere → SOS fires silently, word disappears instantly. Any other typing is safe — only the exact word triggers SOS.

👻 Ghost Mode Free

Guardian X disguises itself as a normal Notepad app. The notepad is real and editable. Hold the screen for 3 seconds to reveal Guardian X. All safety features (shake SOS, check-in alert, panic word) continue running invisibly in the background.

⏹ Stopping SOS — Always Accessible Free

Four ways to stop SOS from anywhere:
  1. Tap ⏹ STOP SOS in the notification shade (fastest — works from lock screen)
  2. Tap STOP on the Guardian X main screen
  3. Tap I'm OK on the Check-In Alert home screen widget
  4. Tap Open App in the notification → then tap STOP on main screen

Ghost Mode: If Guardian X is disguised as a Notepad when SOS fires, tapping the notification or ⏹ STOP SOS button automatically bypasses the Notepad and opens the main screen directly. The STOP button is always reachable — no typing of any password or revealing Ghost Mode manually is needed.

All four paths call SOSManager.killAll() — which cancels the call, silences the siren, stops the flash, clears the notification, and resets all active timers.

📍 Safe Zone Ultra Pro ₹499

Set a GPS radius around your home or office (100m to 5km). If your phone leaves the zone while Check-In Alert is running, a 2-minute warning fires — no response → full SOS.

📡 Live Mutual Tracking Ultra Pro ₹499

When SOS fires, every helper receives an SMS with a live map link. They tap it → browser opens → they share their location → you both see each other in real time. Works in any mobile browser without installing the app.

📞 Fake Call Free Safety Pro ₹249 for unlimited

Simulate a realistic incoming call to escape uncomfortable situations. Set ring delay (mm:ss). Safety Pro: custom caller name and number. Ultra Pro: custom caller photo and ringtone.

🧭 Compass Free

Live distance and bearing to each saved contact. One-tap shortcuts for nearest hospital and police station. Offline navigation arrow for pasted Google Maps links.

🔦 Morse SOS Free

Activates flash and vibration in international SOS Morse code (··· — — — ···). Zero network needed. Works anywhere on Earth.

🎁 Referral Code Free

Each user gets a unique referral code. Share it — when a friend installs via your link, you both get 1 bonus fake call. Codes stack up. After sharing, unlock a 24-hour free trial of all Ultra Pro features.

🌍 Country Detection Free

Auto-detects your country via GPS and SIM card. 168+ countries with correct local emergency numbers for police, ambulance, and fire.
