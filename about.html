Guardian X

Your always-on personal safety guardian

What the App Does

Guardian X is a personal safety app designed for emergencies. One tap — or one shake — sends your GPS location to your emergency contacts, calls them, and activates a loud siren. No account needed. Works offline. Works in 168+ countries.

Core Features

  • One-Tap SOS — calls your contact, sends GPS by SMS, activates siren and flash
  • Shake to SOS — shake 3 times to trigger even with screen locked
  • Dead Man's Switch — set a check-in timer; if you miss it, SOS fires automatically
  • Travel Mode — set arrival time; if you don't confirm safe arrival, SOS fires
  • Fake Call — simulate an incoming call to escape uncomfortable situations
  • Compass — shows distance and direction to each saved contact in real time
  • Ghost Mode — disguise the app as a Notepad so nobody knows it's installed
  • Morse SOS — flash SOS in international Morse code with zero network
  • Offline Navigation — paste a Google Maps link and a compass arrow guides you without internet

Live Mutual Tracking

Ultra Pro users get live location sharing. When SOS fires, helpers receive a link showing the victim on a map. Helpers can share their own location back. Everyone sees everyone else updating in real time.

Privacy

No account. No server. Location goes directly from your phone to your contacts by SMS. Nothing stored by us. Full privacy policy →
