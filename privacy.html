Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Privacy First — Zero Data Collection

What We Collect

Guardian X collects nothing on our servers. We have no user accounts, no analytics servers, and no database of user information.

Location Data

Your GPS location is used only when you trigger an SOS. It is sent directly from your phone to your chosen emergency contacts via SMS. It is never sent to Anthropic, MZN Digital, or any third-party server.

When you use Live Mutual Tracking (Ultra Pro), your location is temporarily stored in a private GitHub Gist (a file associated with your GitHub token). This Gist is automatically deleted when you tap STOP. Only your emergency contacts who have the Gist link can see this data.

Contact Information

Emergency contact names and phone numbers are stored only on your device in app storage. They are never uploaded anywhere.

SMS Messages

SOS messages are sent directly by your phone's SMS system. We do not read, store, or have access to your SMS messages.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. This includes device model, Android version, app version, and the stack trace at the time of a crash. No personal information, location, or contact data is included in crash reports. You can opt out by disabling analytics in your device's Google settings.

Advertising

The free version of Guardian X displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalisation as described in Google's privacy policy. Purchasing any paid plan removes all ads permanently.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Permissions Explained

  • Location — to include GPS coordinates in SOS messages
  • SMS — to send emergency alerts to your contacts
  • Contacts — to let you pick contacts from your phone book
  • Phone — to call your emergency contact when SOS triggers
  • Camera — for the flash strobe SOS signal
  • Notifications — for Dead Man's Switch and Travel Mode alerts

Children's Privacy

Guardian X is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
