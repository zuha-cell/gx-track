Privacy Policy — Guardian X

Last updated: April 2026 | mzndigit0l@gmail.com

1. Who we are

Guardian X is developed and operated by mzndigital. Contact: mzndigit0l@gmail.com

2. What data we collect and why

Location (GPS) — collected only when you trigger SOS, use Safe Zone, or start Guardian X Live sharing. Sent to your nominated emergency contacts. Never stored on our servers. Not collected in background except when Safe Zone geofencing is active.

Emergency contacts — names and phone numbers you enter. Stored only on your device. Never uploaded to our servers.

Device identifiers — your Android ID is used to generate a unique referral code. It is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Usage data — Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports (no personally identifiable information). Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage events (screen views, feature usage). You can opt out in Settings → Privacy.

SOS tracking sessions — when you trigger SOS with live tracking enabled, your location is temporarily written to a GitHub Gist under the app's GitHub token. The Gist is deleted when the SOS session ends. No data is retained after the session.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account information unless you voluntarily submit the beta feedback form
  • We do not record calls, SMS content, or microphone audio
  • We do not track your location continuously — only during active SOS or Live Sharing sessions
  • We do not sell any data to third parties

4. Third-party services

  • Google Play Billing — handles all payment processing. We never see your payment card details.
  • Google AdMob — shows ads to free users. AdMob may use device advertising IDs for ad personalisation. You can reset or opt out of this in your device settings.
  • Firebase (Google) — crash reporting and anonymous analytics.
  • GitHub Gists API — temporary storage for SOS live tracking sessions only.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — map tiles and address search. Your IP address is visible to OSM's tile servers when the map loads.
  • Cloudflare Workers — serves version update checks. No personal data is sent.

5. Permissions and why we need them

  • Location (fine + background) — SOS GPS, Safe Zone geofencing, live tracking
  • Send SMS — emergency alerts to your contacts
  • Call phone — auto-dial emergency contact on SOS
  • Camera — SOS flash/torch signal
  • Microphone — audio for fake call feature
  • Read contacts — contact picker when adding emergency contacts
  • Receive boot completed — restart background services after reboot
  • Notifications — check-in alerts, safe zone breach, permission nudges

6. Children's privacy

Guardian X is designed for adults (13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, contact us at mzndigit0l@gmail.com.

7. EU users — GDPR rights EU

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, for any consent-based processing
  • Right to object — to processing for analytics purposes

Since most data is stored only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights by simply uninstalling the app. For crash/analytics data held by Google Firebase, contact us at mzndigit0l@gmail.com and we will submit a deletion request to Google on your behalf.

Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interests (safety features), consent (analytics and ads), contract performance (subscription features).

8. EU subscriptions — cancellation rights EU

If you purchase a subscription from within the EU, you have a 14-day right of withdrawal (cooling-off period) from the date of purchase, unless you have already used the premium features (in which case the right of withdrawal may be lost under digital content rules). To cancel, go to Google Play → Subscriptions → Guardian X → Cancel.

9. Brazil users BR

We comply with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD). The legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (safety features), consent (analytics), and contract performance (subscriptions). You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data by contacting mzndigit0l@gmail.com.

10. Data retention

  • SOS tracking sessions: deleted immediately when session ends
  • Crash reports: retained by Firebase for 90 days
  • Analytics: aggregated, retained for 14 months (Google's default)
  • Device-side data: retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data

11. Security

Emergency contact data is stored in Android SharedPreferences on your device with standard Android security. We do not transmit contact data to any server. SOS location data is transmitted over HTTPS.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. When we do, we update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or complaints: mzndigit0l@gmail.com

Response time: within 30 days.
