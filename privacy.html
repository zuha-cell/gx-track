Zero Data Collection

What We Collect

Guardian X collects nothing on our servers. No user accounts, no analytics database, no personal information.

Location Data

GPS is used only when you trigger an SOS. It is sent directly from your phone to your emergency contacts by SMS — never to our servers. Live Tracking (Ultra Pro) stores your location temporarily in a private GitHub Gist that is automatically deleted when you tap STOP.

Contacts & SMS

Contact names and numbers are stored only on your device. SMS messages are sent by your phone's own SMS system — we never see them.

Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics for anonymous crash reports (device model, Android version, stack trace). No personal data is included.

Advertising

Free version shows Google AdMob ads. Purchasing any paid plan removes all ads permanently.

Permissions

  • SMS — send emergency alerts
  • Contacts — pick from phone book
  • Phone — call emergency contact on SOS
  • Camera — flash strobe signal
  • Notifications — Dead Man's Switch and Travel Mode alerts
