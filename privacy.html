Emergency SOS — Privacy Policy

✅ No data sold. No tracking. Privacy first.

1. What We Collect

Guardian X collects only what is necessary to provide emergency safety features:

  • Location (GPS) — collected only when SOS is triggered or live tracking is active. Never silently collected in the background.
  • Emergency contacts — names and phone numbers you enter manually. Stored only on your device, never on our servers.
  • Device sensors — accelerometer (shake-to-SOS detection) and compass/magnetometer (compass feature). Values are processed locally and never transmitted.
  • Anonymous ad data — Google AdMob collects anonymous ad interaction data per its own privacy policy.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account information
  • We do not store your location on any server
  • We do not read your SMS history, call logs, or phone contacts beyond what you manually enter in the app
  • We do not sell any data to any third party, ever
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

3. How We Use Your Location

Location is used exclusively to:

  • Include your GPS coordinates in emergency SOS messages sent via SMS to your chosen contacts
  • Generate a live tracking link (Ultra Pro feature) shared only with your emergency contacts when SOS is triggered
  • Detect your country automatically to display the correct local emergency service numbers

Location is never uploaded to our servers. The live tracking link encodes your coordinates directly in the URL — no backend database stores this information. When your contact opens the link, their browser calculates distance locally using OpenStreetMap.

4. Live Tracker Feature (Ultra Pro)

When SOS is triggered with live tracking enabled, a URL is generated containing your GPS coordinates, name, and phone number. This URL is sent via SMS to your emergency contacts only. No server stores this data. The web page uses OpenStreetMap — no Google Maps API key or account is required from the helper.

5. App Permissions — Why We Need Them

  • SEND_SMS — to send emergency SOS alerts to your contacts automatically
  • CALL_PHONE — to directly call emergency contacts and services without the dialler
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — to include GPS coordinates in SOS messages
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to keep shake-to-SOS active when the app is in the background or screen is off
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restart shake monitoring after your phone reboots
  • CAMERA — to activate the flashlight SOS strobe signal
  • VIBRATE — for SOS vibration pattern and Morse code signal
  • READ_CONTACTS — only to let you pick contacts from your phonebook; the app does not copy or upload your contact list

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — serves banner and rewarded video ads. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchases. No payment data is processed by Guardian X directly.
  • OpenStreetMap — used in the live tracking web page to display maps. No personal data is shared with OpenStreetMap.

7. Data Storage and Retention

All data (emergency contacts, app settings, notepad content) is stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences and local storage. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. We retain nothing on servers because Guardian X has no backend servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Guardian X is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time: Android Settings → Apps → Guardian X → Storage → Clear Data.
You can revoke any permission at any time: Android Settings → Apps → Guardian X → Permissions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

2. हम क्या एकत्र नहीं करते

  • हम कोई डेटा नहीं बेचते
  • हम स्थान सर्वर पर संग्रहीत नहीं करते
  • हम SMS, कॉल लॉग या फ़ोन संपर्क सूची नहीं पढ़ते
  • हम किसी तृतीय पक्ष को डेटा नहीं देते

3. स्थान का उपयोग

SOS SMS में GPS निर्देशांक शामिल करने और देश पहचान के लिए केवल उपयोग किया जाता है। डेटा कभी सर्वर पर अपलोड नहीं होता। लाइव ट्रैकर लिंक URL में सीधे निर्देशांक एन्कोड करता है — किसी डेटाबेस में संग्रहीत नहीं।

١. ما الذي نجمعه

يجمع Guardian X فقط ما هو ضروري لتوفير ميزات السلامة في حالات الطوارئ:

  • الموقع (GPS) — يُجمع فقط عند تفعيل SOS أو التتبع المباشر. لا يُجمع أبدًا في الخلفية دون علمك.
  • جهات الاتصال الطارئة — الأسماء وأرقام الهاتف التي تدخلها يدويًا. مخزّنة على جهازك فقط.
  • حساسات الجهاز — مقياس التسارع (كشف الاهتزاز) والبوصلة. تُعالج محليًا ولا تُرسل أبدًا.
  • بيانات الإعلانات — تجمع Google AdMob بيانات تفاعل مجهولة وفقًا لسياسة الخصوصية الخاصة بها.

٢. ما الذي لا نجمعه

  • لا نبيع أي بيانات لأي طرف ثالث إطلاقًا
  • لا نخزّن موقعك على أي خادم
  • لا نقرأ سجل الرسائل أو سجل المكالمات أو قائمة جهات الاتصال
  • لا نتتبعك عبر التطبيقات الأخرى

٣. كيف نستخدم موقعك

يُستخدم الموقع حصريًا لـ:

  • تضمين إحداثيات GPS في رسائل SOS الطارئة المرسلة عبر الرسائل القصيرة
  • إنشاء رابط تتبع مباشر (ميزة Ultra Pro) يُشارك فقط مع جهات الاتصال الطارئة
  • اكتشاف بلدك تلقائيًا لعرض أرقام الطوارئ المحلية الصحيحة

لا يُرفع موقعك إلى خوادمنا أبدًا. يُشفّر رابط التتبع المباشر إحداثياتك مباشرةً في عنوان URL — لا تخزّن قاعدة بيانات خلفية هذه المعلومات.
